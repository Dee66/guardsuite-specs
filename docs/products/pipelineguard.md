

# PipelineGuard (v2.0.0)

Enterprise supply-chain governance, deterministic remediation, compliance ledger, and premium GuardScore inputs for CI/CD workflows and artifact pipelines.


## Overview
PipelineGuard is the premium CI/CD and supply-chain governance blueprint for
enterprise infrastructure and MLOps pipelines. It extends PipelineScan with
advanced workflow-security rules, supply-chain integrity enforcement, a full
compliance ledger, and deterministic FixPack-Lite remediation snippets. Designed
for organizations requiring airtight workflow governance, artifact provenance,
safe secret boundaries, and governance visibility across complex CI/CD systems.
It integrates deeply with GuardBoard, GuardScore, and the Playground ecosystem.
